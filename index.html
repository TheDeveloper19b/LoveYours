Founder & When it was Founded

The brand was established in 2020 by Mr Mfundo Shagala
in a small community located in South Africa in the Zulu
nation province of KwaZulul Natal city of Durban in Shakashead.

Purpose

He started the brand because he wanted to help his community
in terms of youth that wants to have good clothes but cant afford it
.So he made it so that everyone from any family can wear quality clothes
cheap with a brand.

OUR Goal & VISION

Our goal is like climbing a leadder and we are a few steps behind.We want to be one of the best clothing brands in the world we wanna be on top competing with Nike,Adiddas,Puma ...etc
